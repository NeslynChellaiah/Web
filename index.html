INTRODUCTION
    Embellish Events is an Event Management company founded in 2014 . The company aims to serve the intelligent way to plan and turn ideas into actions. It was kick-started by Manickam Kathiresan while he was just 17 who is a very young and energetic Entrepreneur in this modern Era. The company consists of group of 10 members having various departments who works sincerely for the growth and promotions. Manickam who himself is a young person have his overall team with youngsters where he believes that the fresh and young people always possess a raw talent, an immense interest and sincere dedication towards the career. Our company works on ticketed shows, band performances, college shows , artist management, celebrity management of the Indian Cinema. We also organize concerts and conduct the event shows out of the country having the Indian Artists performing the shows to promote the company and also the individual identity.
EVENTS AND PROMOTIONS
    Embellish Events was the first company to produce the ‘Mottamaadi’ music show in association with the Aura groups. Infact, this was our first show that was organized in Trichy and gained a lot of positive vibes. We have also conducted 4 shows in association with 'Theatre Kaaran' groups where one of the shows called ‘Naatuku naalu seidhi’ which gave us a lot of popularity. Theatre Kaaran is a group lasting from long years. We have also conducted a show in Chennai incorporating the famous English series ‘F.R.I.E.N.D.S’ to bring back the old fun for the audience enjoying the show. The event included storytelling, standup comedy, whistling and band performances. We are also doing our 'Open MIKE' shows where one of our shows were set up in Trichy for the first time on the terrace receiving a lot of good audiences. 'CARNATIC 2.0' is a group who set up a show in Trichy by Promotions Partner. This group is owned by 3 persons- Shravan Sridar, Sharanaya Srinivasan and Mahesh whom we are working as a promotion partner with ‘Honeybee Event Management’.
COLLEGE SIDE PROMOTIONS
    We work with many colleges for movie promotions where we had a chance to promote the recent Tamil movie, ‘OH MY KADAVULE’ in one of the best colleges, ETHIRAJ COLLEGE, Chennai. The cast of the movie ‘Rithika Singh and ‘Ashok Selvan’ were invited making the show a huge success. We have also collaborated with ETHIRAJ college as Sponsors to conduct ‘SRISHTI‘ and ‘RHAPSODY’ events(2019-2020). Our other college side collaboration was with ‘SRI BALAJI MEDICAL COLLEGE' as a partner. This gave us an innumerable confidence to conduct more shows in the colleges as the teenagers are always enthusiastic.
COLLOBORATIONS
    Embellish Events is also working with ‘MADRASI KAAPI’ who is a Carnatic beat boxing team where we collaborate together to visit various cities to conduct shows. We have successfully finished our programs in Chennai and Bangalore. Our upcoming shows will be launched in other places like Cochin, Madurai, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune, Trichy, Coimbatore. We also have our upcoming Band shows in several places as well. We have also collaborated with 'FULLY FILMY' and 'PUNPAADU'. For the first time, we have also conducted a dance workshop in Trichy introducing Sabarish, a contestant from 'DANCE VS DANCE' (a television show in COLORS TV, Tamil) which again gained a good response from the audience.
SOCIAL MEDIA PROGRAMS
    On the other side, we have been going live on Instagram with Celebrities, Directors, Aspiring actors, GYM trainers, Newspaper Readers, Singers, Cabin Crews , Digital Marketers and also the Common People. Our aim is to motivate these people in this quarantine lockdown and share their life experiences and the struggle behind their successful journey. We have been getting positive responses from each of the speakers and the audiences.

ABOUT FOUNDER

    Manickam Kathiresan ( born 26 September 1998) is the CEO of Embellish Event Management company. He started his career with few drawing design patterns which were greatly inspired by many people. He gradually started with a small design team who possessed a huge amount of talent in design and explored them further. Also, he was fond of Event Managements hence he started on some real projects. In a short span of time, his interest grew deeply in it which led to the establishment of his own Event Management Company at the very early age of 17. He loves travelling to explore many new places and also he is a great food lover who is interested in tasting the various authentic foods in different places.
